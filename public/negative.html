The Negatives

  • Isolation: Social Isolation is an absence of social relationships, a.k.a "being lonely". Social Isolation can lead to:

    • Avoiding social contact due to shame and depression
    • Severe distress
    • Lack of important social relationships
    • Have social anxiety

  • Using technology can be very damaging to your eyes, it can create some vision problmes such as:

    • Blurred vision
    • Dry eyes
    • Eye strain

  • Hearing Loss, a.k.a tinnitus. Tinnitus is the ringing in the ears, it happens when you listen to something that is too loud for too long, it is a sign that you are losing your ability to hear.

  • Distractions:

    • Even w/o technology, the time span that teenagers can pay attention without distractions is 3 minutes
    • The sound notifications make and the ringing of the phone are one of the main causes for distractions for the average teen
