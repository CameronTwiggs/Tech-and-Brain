The Negatives

  • Sense of Isolation: Social isolation is an absence of social relationships, a.k.a "being lonely". It can lead to:

    • Depression
    • Social Anxiety
    • Spending too much time indoors
    • Severe distress
    • Lack important social skills and relationships

  • Vision Problems: Using technology too much can be very damaging to your eyes. Screens give out something called blue lights which can cause:

    • Eye strain
    • Dry eyes
    • Blurred vision

  • Hearing Loss (a.k.a tinnitus) is the ringing in the ears, you usually experience it after listening to something too loud for too long, whiplash, too much ear wax, or medication side effects. Around 15 to 20 percent of people in the world are affected by tinnitus

  • Distractions:

    • Even w/o technology, the time span that teenagers can pay attention without distractions is 3 minutes
    • The sound notifications make and the ringing of the phone are one of the main causes for distractions for the average teen
